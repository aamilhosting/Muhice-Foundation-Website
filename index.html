    Our Mission and Goals

    Muhice Foundation is an organization formed by close friends and relatives to preserve the memory of TK Muhammad Hussain, who was an active cultural activist of Velliyur. The main areas of activity are educational advancement of rural students, nation reconstruction through poverty alleviation, women empowerment and human resource development.
